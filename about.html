History

The founder of Limprove went back to campus in 2010 and studied at The George Washington University. As a part of his coursework, he worked on some software projects by individual or as a member of team with his classmates. They hoped to have a place to exhibit their work. This is the origination of Limlabs, named after Limprove.

Currently, Limlabs acts as a forum for the development team of Limprove, where they can share and discuss anything about their work.

Even the best can be better! Let's improve to make it better!
